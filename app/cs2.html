Why this beats an odds-anchored AI (e.g. bo3.gg)

  • No market input. bo3.gg ingests live bookmaker odds, which compress toward 50% and carry vig — so it under-weights lopsided structural edges. We use none, so a true mismatch isn't dragged back to a coin-flip.
  • Damped, not stacked. Naively summing form+H2H+rank produces runaway 85%+ calls (our first pass hit 89.5% for M80). We capped H2H and damped weights to land a disciplined 66%.
  • Event-aware form. Generic 3-month form misses "HEROIC is collapsing at this event." We add an event-form term.
  • Honest on BO1. The model widens on high-variance BO1 rather than faking precision.
